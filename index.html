INTRO

Introduction Note

The scientific-audio journal R.A.M.A, consisting of students specializing in Technology, Art and Production and Content Management, strives to provide the best and latest content in the field of Information and Communication Technology to science lovers.

"Our mission here at R.A.M.A. is to make the scientific concepts more clear for enthusiasts by presenting them in a modern and novel ways."

Pouya Danandeh - Founder & Former CEO

Boring... Boring ... Just Boring...

"It was about a long time ago.... I was just tired of association's common planes and student's routine ideas in our university. All I could see, were people drowned in their phone and PC. So, I realized the virtual space and personal systems were the best way to inject my favorite scientific concepts to their brain; but, not by using usual and common ways like cold and incomprehensible journals. I would like to do it by utilizing novel methods. Eventually, I discussed my idea with the then Scientific Association of Computer Science's secretary."
